1. Holiday to Bromo

Dengan tulisan bahasa Inggris

In June 2018, my family and I went on vacation to East Java.

Gambar 1: Pegunungan
Gambar 2: Matahari terbit / Sunrise

First, we departed from Semarang to Batu Malang by car. Arriving there, we immediately rested at the hotel for a while and went to eat at the Batu Malang square. The next day we went to Jawa Timur Park 3, the place is nice and there are lots of interesting photo spots there. Miniatures of Japanese cities, Dutch cities, India and many more. After that we returned to the hotel to rest for a while because at dawn we had to prepare for Bromo. At 1 am we got up and got ready to go by jeep. We had to get up very early because we had to catch the sunrise. The trip from the hotel to Bromo is quite far and takes as much as …. o'clock. We got there at exactly 4am and the weather was very, very cold. Our first destination was the Peak of Mount Bromo. The view is amazing, I can see 3 Bromo mountains and the sunrise. After that, we went to the next destination, namely Whispering Sand. There we took a lot of photos. And our last destination is Teletubbies Hill. When it was noon we went back to town and had lunch there.

Actually my family and I were very tired because of the trip but the beautiful scenery we saw was really worth it. It's an unforgettable experience.

After walking for a while and buying souvenirs in the city of Malang, we returned to Semarang.

Catatan:
- Batu Malang: Kota di Jawa Timur
- Kata Coach di Eagle School, nanti akan berencana untuk retreat ke Batu Malang, untuk karya wisata.
- Coach Dedie dan Nana sudah pergi ke Gunung Bromo di bulang Agustus, langit dan pemandangannya bagus.

2. About Me Devan - Descriptive Text

Dengan tulisan bahasa Inggris

Judul: About Me Devan

My name is Devan. I am senior high school. I like Math Computer and music.

The subject I like the most is Mathematics. Since I was a kid, my math scores were the highest compared to the other subjects. I have participated in many competitions. Competitions between schools, at the Mall, school's Open House and at the Olympic level. Sometimes I win, sometimes I don't. The championship that was most memorable for me when I was in grade 5 elementary school, for the OSN level, I entered the semifinals; and recently I won Olympic gold at the provincial level of Central Java and Jogja. This further broadens my horizons, further motivates me to be even more active and diligent in practicing and participating in various competitions in order to improve my mathematical abilities.

Beside Math, I also like computer programs, namely python. Moreover, at Eagle School there is an IT talent class. There I can apply my hobby regarding this computer program. I started making math programs. And those are my projects in the IT talent class. Python is a coding programming language. Coding is very necessary and important in today's IT field. Everything uses coding. For that, I really want to deepen my knowledge of coding, such as Python, HTML, and Javascript. Because later it will be needed now and in the future. In the future, I will make projects other than math, such as my own portfolio and web.

Apart from mathematics and IT, I also like to play piano. I have been taking piano lessons for 3 years. Several times from my piano lessons, I held small concerts at the Semarang mall. This is one that made me bolder and memorize some of the songs I played. In my spare time at home, I often play songs taught in piano lessons.

My hope is I would like to take part in competitions in mathematics, both at the city and national levels during my 3 years at this high school. And also continue to deepen the capabilities of the Python program. Because at this time, Mathematics and python are related to each other. My dream is to be a mathematician and programmer. Hopefully with the talent that God has given me, I can develop it with effort and of course only with God's help.

4. Retreat Eagle School 2023

Kamis, 8 Sep 2023

Berangkat ke Lembah Kemenangan
Lokasi: Lembah Kemenangan, Ungaran, Kab. Semarang

Hari ini siswa SMA dan gurunya pergi dari sekolah naik bus, dengan membawa 2 tas yang berat, seperti tas buku dan Sekolah, lalu masukkan ke dalam bawah bus. Nanti akan ke Lembah Kemenang an melewati, Jalan Tol di Ungaran, makan siang. Ada 3 sesi hari ini.

SESI 1 ~ 12:30-15:30
Pada sesi pertama pengenalan retreat yaitu kelompok yel-yel dan kamar, dikumpulkan hp, dan juga ibadah bersama pendeta Michael. Setelah itu, nyari kamar tapi malah ke atas jadi bingung, akhirnya ada temannya yang bantuin ke kamar, dio dalamnya ada Gilbert, dan mandi dengan membawa peralatan mandi dan bawa baju ganti, juga belum terlalu terbiasa jadi agak bingung.

SESI 2 ~ 16:30-18:30
Pada sesi kedua, kembali ke aula dikumpulkan hp lagi ke kotaknya, ada ibadah bersama pendeta Hengky, khotbah-nya marah-marah dan berdoanya kaut untuk menjauh dari dosa, setelah itu makan malam ada ayamnya, suasananya rame, dan melihat coach Ditus yang sedang berbicara sama Ps. Hengky.

SESI 3 ~ 19:15-22:00
Pada sesi ketiga, ada ibadah bersama Sr. Ditus, tentang kekudusan, juga ada sesi tanya jawab (Q&A). Dan kegiatan terakhir hari ini adalah latihan yel-yel dengan tema Be Excellent jam 10 malam, karena banyak siswa yang melanggar tata tertib, maka C. Gaby dan C. Agus S marah dan menegurnya, seperti membersihkan meja makan. Di malam hari akhirnya melihat penampilan kelompok yel-yel dekat api unggun karena abis latihan. Baru tidur di kamar Graha Gita sekiar tengah malam atau jam 12 malam, sempat main hp dan menunjukkan ke Gilbert.

Jumat, 9 Sep 2023

Kegiatan Outbound
Di pagi hari yaitu jam setengah 6 pagi (05:30), kita akan pergi ke ruang aula untuk beribadah, dan lalu sarapan. Habis itu, ada beberapa kegiatan outbound yang menarik, sama Daniel T pada kelompok yel-yel yang sama. Yang pertama bermain bola dan pipa, agar pipa ga bocor, maka dipegang. Selanjutnya, memasukkan bola ke dalam lubangnya, dengan pegangan orang-orang dari kelompok, dan ada permainan melompat di lingkaran.

Kegiatan Terakhir saat Retreat
Setelah itu, baru mandi dan sarapan makan nasi goreng. Baru melihat pengumuman dari hasil kegiatan permainan outbound dengan banyak hadiah sebelum pulang. Lalu pulangnya naik bis dan kembali ke sekolah Eagle School.
